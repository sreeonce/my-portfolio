TaskFlow AI

A smart AI-powered productivity system that transforms long-term goals into structured daily tasks with dynamic scheduling, prioritization, and adaptive planning.

The Problem

People struggle to convert long-term goals into consistent daily action.

Traditional to-do apps focus only on task listing, not execution planning or prioritization.

As a result, users often lose track of goals, underestimate workload, and fail to maintain consistency.

Why We Built This

Bridging the gap between goals and daily execution using AI.

TaskFlow AI was built to explore how artificial intelligence can assist in planning and decision-making.

The goal was to create a system that not only tracks tasks but actively helps structure them into achievable daily plans.

The Approach

Building a system that connects goals, planning, and execution

TaskFlow AI breaks down long-term goals into smaller actionable tasks using structured planning logic and AI-assisted decomposition. It dynamically prioritizes tasks based on urgency, workload, and deadlines.

Challenges & What We Learned

Building a system that balances structured planning with flexible AI-driven adjustments was a key challenge.

AI Logic
Designing meaningful task breakdowns without making the output too rigid or unrealistic.
Scheduling
Handling dynamic re-prioritization while keeping user plans stable and understandable.
UX Design
Making complex planning feel simple and intuitive for everyday users.
