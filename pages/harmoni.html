The Concept

Workplace stress is common. Support for it isn't.

Most workplaces talk about wellbeing but rarely build anything that actually helps someone in the middle of a stressful week. There's no easy on-ramp, nothing that meets employees where they are, between meetings, mid-deadline, right when stress is highest.

Harmoni was designed to be that on-ramp. A simple, welcoming entry point that gets someone signed up and into a short mindfulness practice before they've had time to talk themselves out of it.

Design Highlights

Making wellness easy to actually start.

The design is built around lowering the barrier between "I should probably do something about this stress" and actually doing it.

01
Guided mindfulness practices
A curated set of short, structured practices designed to fit into an actual workday, not replace it.
02
Frictionless signup
A quick, welcoming signup flow designed to get people started right away, instead of losing them at the door.
03
Calm, workplace-friendly visuals
A soft, unhurried visual language that feels appropriate to open at a desk, without feeling like a wellness app pretending to be serious software.
04
Personalised suggestions
Practices are suggested based on what the user needs in the moment, rather than one generic list for everyone.
